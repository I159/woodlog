WoodLog is a logger downed from wood.

WoodLog is ridiculously simple structured and leveled logger based on stdlib `log` package. WoodLog is not lightning fast neither it doesn't produce extra complicated logs structure. It does minimum what logger must do - L.O.G.S (!) with structure and levels.
